Met Links
Master Plan

East Providence, Rhode Island

The Met Links restoration plan focused on preserving the historic Donald Ross front nine while creating a sustainable and exceptional nine-hole golf experience. The Northeast Golf Company worked closely with Marshall Properties to conserve the essence of the original holes while integrating new design elements that complement the landscape and honor the course's storied past.
